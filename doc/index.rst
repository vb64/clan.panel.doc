Welcome to Clan-panel WOT's documentation!
==========================================

Clan-panel it is a web-site, intended to help WorldOfTanks clan administrators (leader, deputies, recruiters, commanders) to solve typical problems:

* track clan events
* clan vehicles accounting
* member activity accounting
* create and manage clan companies
* manage of attacks and defense of Stronghold

Clan-panel serves the following clusters of World Of Tanks:

* `Russia`_
* `Europe`_
* `North America`_
* `Southeast Asia`_
